Midnight Nutella Dip

Description

Delicious and easy-to-cook dessert.

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons of Nutella or any other choco-hazelnut spread
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients in a small microwave-safe container.
  2. Heat in microwave oven for 1 minute.
  3. Stir the dip with a toothpick until somewhat smooth; let cool for 2 minutes before eating.

Note

For a thicker sauce, use cream instead of milk. For a thinner consistency, add more milk after cooking and stir. For a thicker consistency, add more Nutella after cooking and stir.
